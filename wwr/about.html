Greys River Rafting Guides

Our goal is to provide the most thrilling and safe rafting adventure in Wyoming. We hope you join us for not only the most beautiful scenery, but the greatest thrill of your life.

History

Greys River Rafting Guides began as a group of fishing buddies that wanted to share their love and knowledge of the outdoors. Now we are seasoned professionals who provide top-notch experiences.

Each of our guides have years of experience, this ensures that every adventure is safe and enjoyable for all skill levels.
