White Water Rafting

White water rafting is an outdoor recreational activity involving navigating an inflatable raft down a river, particularly through sections with rapids. These rapids are caused by fast-moving water flowing over rocks and obstacles, creating turbulence and varying levels of difficulty, often graded on a scale from Class I to Class VI
