Our Mission

Since our founding in 1994, our mission has remained unchanged. We believe that adventure is for ALL.

The Greys river flows through the Wyoming mountain range to the town of Alpine. We work with the local community to enrich businesses and preserve the surrounding nature.

What We Offer

Choose from half-day family-friendly floats, full-day expeditions with riverside lunches, or multi-day camping adventures under the stars. We also customize trips for corporate events, celebrations, and group bonding. Guided by Leave No Trace principles, we protect the wild places we explore.
